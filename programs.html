Programs

Bachelor of Civil Engineering

09.03.2012 by VIA

Our Bachelor Programme of Civil Engineering is a 3½-year full-time programme taught in English. It provides you with skills and knowledge needed to manage structural calculations, drawings, soil mechanics, road design and calculations, etc. The flexibility of the programme and the variety of electives allow you to create the degree that will prepare you for the career you want.

Civil engineering is an international profession that involves the design, construction, operation and improvement of the built environment. Civil engineers provide the technical expertise and management skills to plan, design, construct and maintain such facilities as buildings, bridges, water supply systems, waste treatment systems, road and rail networks, air and sea ports in a sustainable manner to reduce the impact on the environment.

The teaching philosophy implies a combination of theory lessons, assignments and project work predominantly carried out in groups of three to five students.

Bachelor of Global Business Engineering

09.03.2012 by VIA

Global Business Engineering is a full-degree bachelor programme taught in English. The attractiveness of the global business engineer rests on a unique blend of science & technology, economics & international marketing combined with languages & culture.

Engineering courses account for about two-thirds of the study programme. Business and language courses make up the last third of the programme.

As a graduate, you can look forward to excellent employment prospects worldwide. You are likely to find job opportunities within design and implementation of technical solutions as well as within marketing and sales. Typically, you may work as a project manager, a consulting engineer or as an export or sales manager.

The interaction between Danish and international students combined with further studies and work placements inside or outside of Denmark will give you invaluable professional and cultural experience before graduating. This means that you can leave with the confidence that you have already gained part of the practical experience employers are looking for.

Bachelor of ICT Engineering

09.03.2012 by VIA

The programme’s profile also covers a number of main trends in the information and communication technology field, such as embedding information technology in intelligent products for the home, within transport technology and in industrial automation processes as well as the integration of administrative and industrial systems.

The goal is to educate Information and Communication Technology Engineers and give them comprehensive insight into software, hardware and technological development as well as to ensure that they can communicate and work with other stakeholders in the industry.

Information and Communication Technology Engineers typically find employment in software houses, automation companies, large and medium-sized companies as Software Engineers, Systems Consultants and Project Managers.

The first four semesters are compulsory, which means class attendance is required. If a student fails to comply with the required level of study activity and have no legal excuse, such as medical documentation in case of sickness, etc., the student will be withdrawn from the programme.
